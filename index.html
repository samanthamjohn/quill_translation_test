Combining Describing Sentences

When two sentences describe the same person, you can combine them into one sentence. Use and to connect the describing words.


You See

She is kind.

She is happy.


You Write

She is kind and happy.

The past tense of do is did.

The past tense of go is went.

 Activity prompt

Using Can

To ask a question with can, put can first, the person second, and the action word third.


You See

___ ___ ___ peanuts?


You Write

Can she eat peanuts?

Usando Can

Para hacer una pregunta con can, coloca can primero, la persona segundo y la palabra de acción tercero.


Tú Ves

___ ___ ___ peanuts?


Tú Escribe

Can she eat peanuts?

 Utilizar Can
